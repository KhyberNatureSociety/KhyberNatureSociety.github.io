Opportunities & Careers

Join us in protecting northern Pakistan's wildlife through volunteer work, research internships, or active positions.

Khyber Nature Society welcomes expressions of interest from students, volunteers, researchers, field assistants, wildlife staff, and conservation professionals who want to contribute to biodiversity conservation in northern Pakistan.

We accept member and volunteer interest on a rolling basis. Please share your details and area of interest, and our team will contact you when a suitable opportunity, activity, or role becomes available.

Volunteer With Us

Khyber Nature Society welcomes volunteers interested in field learning, awareness activities, biodiversity documentation, community outreach, photography, media, and conservation education.
